# Domain 5 Exam Patterns

- Lost exact values after summarization: persistent case-facts block.
- Missed middle findings: reorder or summarize key findings at top.
- Huge tool outputs: trim relevant fields.
- Multi-source conflict: preserve provenance and uncertainty.
- Stale resumed session: fresh session with summary and targeted re-read.
