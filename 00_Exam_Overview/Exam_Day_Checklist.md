# Exam Day Checklist

## Before Booking

- Complete the official practice exam at least twice.
- Score 900+ in practice before sitting the real exam.
- Review all six scenarios, not just the four you expect.
- Read the official exam guide end to end.
- Drill the top anti-patterns until the wrong answers feel obvious.

## On Exam Day

- Read the final sentence first: BEST fix, MOST effective first step, or root cause.
- Do not over-index on one keyword; understand the failure mode.
- Mark and return to questions with two plausible answers.
- If an option adds a new component, ask whether the simpler architecture directly solves the root cause.
- If a question uses guarantee, compliance, financial action, or security boundary language, look for code-level enforcement.
