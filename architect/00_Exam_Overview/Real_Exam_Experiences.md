# Real Exam Experiences

## Patterns Reported By Candidates

- The real exam is more scenario-heavy than memorization-heavy.
- Options are plausible. Partial fixes are common distractors.
- Agentic architecture, MCP tool design, and Claude Code configuration details are frequently tested.
- The exam rewards production judgment: root-cause analysis, safe failure handling, and reliable boundaries.

## Practical Advice

- Build or mentally simulate at least two systems: a customer support agent and a structured extraction pipeline.
- Practice explaining why the wrong options are wrong.
- Treat every question as a production incident: what broke, why did it break, and what control prevents it next time?
