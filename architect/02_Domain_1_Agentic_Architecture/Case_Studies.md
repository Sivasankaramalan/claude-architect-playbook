# Case Studies

## Multi-Agent Research

Coordinator decomposes topic, spawns search and document agents, passes structured findings to synthesis, checks coverage gaps, then asks a report agent to format the final answer.

## Customer Support

Agent identifies customer, looks up order, applies policy, escalates ambiguous or high-risk cases, and uses gates for refunds or account actions.
