# Publication Checklist

Before publishing or opening a PR, confirm:

- [ ] No real exam questions, dumps, or copied practice-bank content.
- [ ] No employer, client, or private training material.
- [ ] No raw PDFs, PPTX files, DOCX files, videos, or private HTML course exports.
- [ ] No emails, tokens, credentials, private URLs, or internal system names.
- [ ] All practice questions are original and educational.
- [ ] Root and track READMEs clearly state these are unofficial community study guides.
- [ ] New content lands in the correct track (`architect/` or `developer/`).

Recommended public framing: Claude certification study hub with separate Architect and Developer tracks.
